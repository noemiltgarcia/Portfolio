About Me

A Texas chica with a passion for visual design and UI/UX design.

My design journey started during my college years as a Computer Science undergrad, where I explored a graphic design minor and fell in love with creating logos, brand identities, wireframes, and prototypes. Design quickly became my niche, and I've had the opportunity to work with competitions, religious organizations, and start-ups, honing my skills along the way.

Currently, I'm pursuing my post baccalaureate in Visual Communication and on the lookout for exciting internship opportunities in the design industry. I thrive in a collaborative environment and pride myself on excellence, perseverance, diligence, and empathy in my work. For me, design is all about people - both the ones who create it and the people it's created for. My goal is to create designs that bring joy, spark curiosity, develop knowledge, and captivate audiences through clean and simple aesthetics.

Outside of the design bubble, I'm a well-rounded individual with a zest for life. I'm passionate about my faith and serving others, and I value time spent with family and friends. I'm always up for jamming and dancing to music, getting lost in a good book, and embarking on new adventures to explore different places, languages, cultures, hobbies, foods, and entertainment.

If you're looking for a talented and dedicated designer with a diverse skill set and a genuine love for what I do, I'd love to chat with you about how I can contribute to your team! Let's create something amazing together.
